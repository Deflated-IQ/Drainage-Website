Drainage Problems

Signs You Have a Blocked Drain

Don't ignore a blocked drain — it can quickly worsen and cause costly water damage. These are the most common warning signs:

  • Water draining slowly from sink, bath or shower
  • Gurgling sounds from drains or toilets
  • Unpleasant smells from drains or outside manholes
  • Water backing up into the bath when flushing toilet
  • Puddles forming around outdoor drain covers
  • Repeatedly blocked toilet that won't flush properly
  • Raised water level in outdoor manhole covers

⚡ Acting quickly saves money. A small blocked drain cleared today costs far less than water damage or pipe collapse tomorrow.

Common Causes of Blocked Drains in Oxford

🍳

Grease & Fat

Cooking grease poured down kitchen sinks builds up over time, narrowing and eventually blocking drain pipes. Extremely common in Oxford homes.

🌿

Tree Roots

Oxford's mature tree-lined streets mean root intrusion is a top cause of blocked drains and blocked manholes. CCTV surveys reveal the extent.

🧻

Wet Wipes & Foreign Objects

Wet wipes — even "flushable" ones — are a leading cause of blocked toilets and sewer blockages. Only flush the 3 Ps.

🍂

Leaves & Debris

Outdoor drain blocked? Leaves, silt and garden debris commonly block outdoor drains and gullies, especially in autumn.
