Our Repair Services

Drain Repair Services in Oxford

We carry out all types of drain repairs for Oxford homeowners, landlords and businesses. Most structural repairs follow a CCTV drain survey to accurately identify the problem.

🩹

Drain Patch Repairs

Drain patch repairs (also called patch lining) are a no-dig solution to fix localised cracks, fractures and joint defects inside drain pipes. A resin patch is inserted and cured in place — no excavation required. Faster and more cost-effective than full pipe replacement.

🔄

Drain Relining

Full drain relining involves inserting a new fabric liner coated with resin into the existing pipe and inflating it to form a new pipe within the old one. Ideal for extended sections of cracked or degraded drainpipe — no digging, no disruption.

🚧

Drain Excavation & Replacement

Where pipe damage is too severe for no-dig methods, we carry out targeted excavation and drain pipe replacement. We minimise disruption, working quickly and reinstating surfaces to a high standard.

🛡

Drain Maintenance

Annual drain maintenance checks include CCTV inspection, jetting and a written condition report. Ideal for landlords, letting agents and property managers wanting to stay ahead of drain problems in Oxford.

Signs You Need Drain Repairs

Does Your Drain Need Repairing?

Structural drain damage is often invisible from the surface. These signs suggest your drains may need a repair rather than just a clear-out:

  • Recurring blocked drains despite regular clearing
  • Persistent slow drainage after jetting
  • Wet patches or sinkholes appearing in your garden
  • Cracks appearing in walls or paving near drain runs
  • Strong sewage smell from gardens or under floors
  • Subsidence or ground movement near drainage
  • Roots repeatedly entering your drains
