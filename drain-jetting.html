What Is Drain Jetting?

The Most Powerful Drain Cleaning Method

Drain jetting (also called high-pressure drain jetting or water jetting) uses pressurised water — up to 4,000 PSI — to blast through blockages and scour the inside of your drain pipe clean.

Unlike drain rods that simply push a blockage, high-pressure jetting completely removes grease, scale, silt and root matter from the pipe walls, leaving your drain thoroughly cleaned and clear.

  • Removes grease, fat and scale from pipe walls
  • Cuts through and removes tree roots
  • Clears silt and debris from manholes
  • Safe for all standard drain pipe materials
  • Suitable for domestic and commercial drains
  • Often prevents further blockages for years

When Is Drain Jetting Needed?

🍳

Severe Grease Blockages

Kitchen drains in Oxford homes and restaurants can accumulate years of grease. Only high-pressure jetting removes it completely rather than just pushing it further down the pipe.

🌿

Root Removal from Drains

Root removal from drains requires the cutting power of high-pressure jetting. We blast roots clear and can follow up with a CCTV survey to assess pipe condition.

🔵

Scale & Mineral Deposits

Hard water areas like Oxfordshire lead to limescale build-up in drains and pipes. Drain jetting removes scale and restores pipe bore.

🔄

Recurring Blocked Drains

If your drain keeps blocking, a full jetting clean often resolves the underlying build-up that is causing repeated problems.
