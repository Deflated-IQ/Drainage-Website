What Is a CCTV Drain Survey?

See Inside Your Drainage System

A CCTV drain survey (also known as a CCTV camera survey or drain camera survey) is the most accurate way to inspect the inside of your drains and sewers. A high-definition camera is inserted into the drain and pushed through the pipe, transmitting live footage of the pipe's interior.

Our drainage engineers then review the footage, identify any defects and provide you with a full written drainage survey report, often on the same day. The camera footage can be recorded to USB or DVD for your records or for your solicitor.

  • Identifies blockages, root intrusion, cracks and collapses
  • No excavation needed — non-invasive inspection
  • Full written report provided same day
  • HD camera footage recorded to USB/DVD
  • Used for homebuyer surveys, insurance claims and maintenance
  • Available across Oxford and all of Oxfordshire

What Does Our CCTV Survey Find?

🌿

Tree Root Intrusion

Oxford's mature trees are a leading cause of drain damage. CCTV identifies roots before they cause serious structural damage.

💔

Cracked or Fractured Pipes

Subsidence, ground movement and age can crack drain pipes. CCTV finds cracks before they become costly collapses.

🚧

Collapsed or Misaligned Drains

We identify sections where drains have collapsed, displaced or been misaligned, which cause persistent blockages and flooding.

🔗

Illegal Connections

We identify surface water draining into the foul sewer — a common issue in Oxford that can create compliance problems and surcharging.

🔵

Blockages & Build-Up

Grease, scale, silt and debris accumulation that is causing slow drainage or recurring blockages is identified precisely.
